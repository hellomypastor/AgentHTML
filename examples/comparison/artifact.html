Synthesis

Recommendation

Redis is the strongest default for most platform teams at this scale. Its data-structure versatility eliminates secondary stores for rate limiting, sessions, and leader election, which compounds into lower total system complexity despite higher per-node ops burden.

  • Choose Memcached if your access pattern is strictly get/set on opaque blobs and your team wants minimal moving parts in the cache tier.
  • Choose Cloudflare KV if the product is edge-first (static config distribution, feature flags, A/B assignments) and you have no existing infrastructure team.
